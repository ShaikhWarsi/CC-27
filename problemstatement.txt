2. Phishing Awareness Website (By WiCYS Club)
Problem Statement
Many users fall victim to phishing attacks due to lack of awareness. Build a robust
website that helps users identify phishing messages.
Objective
● Spread awareness about phishing attacks
● Help users identify fake messages or emails

End Product
● A static or dynamic website
● Shows examples of real vs fake emails/messages
● Simple quiz or checklist for identification

Cybersecurity Angle
● Phishing attacks, social engineering basics

Source
● Awareness-based problem statements from Cyber Awareness Hackathons
● Used in NASSCOM student cybersecurity events
